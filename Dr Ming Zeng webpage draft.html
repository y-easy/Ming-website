About Me

Dr Ming is a Specialist GP who graduated from University of New South Wales and subsequently worked at Bankstown-Lidcombe Hospital and Campbelltown Hospital. She received her Fellowship of the Royal Australian College of General Practitioners in 2019, recognising her postgraduate specialist qualifications in general practice.

She loves being a GP, providing holistic care and building long-term relationships with her patients. She has an interest in child health, antenatal care, preventative medicine and chronic disease management. She strongly believes in empowering patients with knowledge and education.

Raised and educated in Sydney, she has fond memories of growing up in the Ryde area. She loves working at North Ryde Medical Centre where she can give back to the local community.

In her spare time, she enjoys baking, going for walks and spending time with her family and friends.

Dr Ming is accepting new patients.

Dr Ming Zeng (ABN 52 423 194 511) operates as an independent General Practitioner doctor consulting at the North Ryde Medical Centre facility. She is not an employee of the Practice, and she maintains full professional autonomy in the treatment of her patients.
