About Me

Dr Ming is a Specialist GP who graduated from University of New South Wales with Distinction and subsequently worked at Bankstown-Lidcombe Hospital and Campbelltown Hospital. She has worked in general practice in Winston Hills and Richmond, where she gained invaluable experience through caring for a wide variety of patients and undertaking nursing home visits, home visits and after hours on call. She received her Fellowship of the Royal Australian College of General Practitioners in 2019, recognising her postgraduate specialist qualifications in general practice. She loves being a GP, providing holistic care and building long-term relationships with her patients. She has an interest in child health, antenatal care, preventative medicine and chronic disease management. Dr Ming also has a passion for medical education as she has taught and mentored medical students throughout her university and working years. Similarly, she strongly believes in empowering patients with knowledge and education. Raised and educated in Sydney, she has fond memories of growing up in the Ryde area. She is excited about working at North Ryde Medical Centre where she can give back to the local community. In her spare time, she enjoys baking, growing succulents and spending time with her family and friends. Please note that Dr Ming is currently not taking new patients.

Fees

  • Standard Consult (15min) - up front fee $85 with $41.20 Medicare rebate, $43.80 out of pocket.
  • Long Consult (20-30 minutes)- Up front fee $150 with $79.70 Medicare rebate, $70.30 out of pocket.
  • Extended consult (over 40 minutes) - Up front fee $200 with $117.40 Medicare rebate, $82.60 out of pocket.
  • Antenatal Consult - Up front fee $105 with $43.95 Medicare rebate, $61.05 out of pocket.

I offer telehealth services both over telephone or video. Please note for telephone consults there is only a Medicare rebate for a standard consult available so if your telephone consult goes over you will be charged based on the time but the Medicare rebate remains the same - $41.20 so the out of pocket costs rises. All Chronic Disease Management Plans and 75+ Health assessments incur no out of pocket cost. Some procedures incur additional costs: Implanon - please make an initial standard (15 Minute) appointment for planning and organising prescription insertion $150 (+ $80 initial 15 minutes planning appointment) replacement or removal $200 (+ $80 initial 15 minutes planning appointment) Skin lesion removals - vary depending on many factors such as what part of the body it is removed from please make an initial 15 minute consult to discuss.
